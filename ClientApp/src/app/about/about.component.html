College Store
  •   Description
The idea behind this application will be to help international student either sale or give away freely the items they own, to fellow students who will continue to stay in Canada or new coming students. These action will take place on a desktop application called College-Store or CS for short. This application is limited to only students, allowing students to login to the application using their respective college IDs
